DESCRIPTION:

EPITECH is a beautifully crafted  portfolio website . It is modern, 
trendy and features a visually attractive design. Epitech has all the important elements of an effective 
website: an awesome fullscreen hero banner, about and qualification section, services, 
portfolio, testimonial and contact section. Epitech is also mobile and retina ready. 
It will look great on any devices from mobile to desktop and on any screen resolutions.
